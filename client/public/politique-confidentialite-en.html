2Minimum Age and Protection of Minors

⚠️ Minimum age required

Use of Positive Affirmations is reserved for persons aged 13 and over. For users under 18, parental or legal guardian consent is required before using the app, providing personal data, or making a Premium purchase.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from persons under the age of 13. If you are under 13, you must not use this application.

3Data Protection Principles

We are committed to upholding the following principles:

  • Lawfulness, fairness and transparency: We will always require your consent to process your data and will inform you in a transparent manner.
  • Data minimisation: We only collect data that is strictly necessary.
  • Storage limitation: Your data will only be retained for as long as necessary.
  • Integrity and confidentiality: We take all necessary measures to ensure the security of your data.

4Data Collected

4.1 Data stored locally (on your device)

The following data is stored solely on your device and is never sent to our servers:

  • Your first name
  • Your preferred language
  • Your favourite affirmations
  • Your browsing history within the app
  • Your app preferences
  • Your submitted contributions (proposed quotes and affirmations), stored locally until deleted

✨ Important: This data remains solely on your device. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, this information will be permanently lost.

4.2 Data collected when submitting contributions

When you use the "Submit Your Own" feature to propose a quote or affirmation, we collect:

  • The text of the quote or affirmation voluntarily submitted
  • The content type (affirmation or quote)
  • The author's name (optional, if you choose to provide it)
  • The date and time of submission

📧 Transmission: This data is sent by email via the EmailJS service to kcevent37@gmail.com for moderation purposes. No personally identifiable data (name, email, IP address) is associated with these submissions.

✅ What we do NOT collect when you submit a contribution

Your identity, email address, phone number or any other personal information is not required to submit a contribution. Submission is voluntary and anonymous.

4.3 Data collected for the Premium version

Only if you subscribe to the Premium version:

  • Email address: To link your subscription to your account
  • RevenueCat user ID: For purchase management via Google Play
  • Subscription and expiry dates: To manage the validity of your Premium access
  • Payment status: Payment confirmation via Google Play Billing

4.4 Technical data collected automatically

  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • Mobile advertising identifier (IDFA/AAID) for Google AdMob
  • App usage data (pages visited, duration)

Retained until you delete it (by uninstalling the app, clearing app data, or wiping the cache).
